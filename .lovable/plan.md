
## Techlab Solars — Energy Infrastructure Website

### Overview
A 5-page premium dark-mode website repositioning Techlab Solars as an Energy Infrastructure Powerhouse. Industrial Futurism design system with `#050505` background, `#FFB800` amber accent, Outfit + Inter typography.

---

### Page 1 — Home
- **Full-screen hero** with cinematic dark industrial/solar Unsplash image, "ENERGY INFRASTRUCTURE — INDIA" label, "DOMINATE THE GRID." headline, subtext, and two CTA buttons (Request Site Survey / View Projects)
- **Scrolling metrics ticker strip** — 25.4 MW DEPLOYED, 12,400 TONS CO₂ SAVED, CHENNAI HQ, etc. with subtle top/bottom borders
- **Bento grid** — Large card (Grid-Ready Solar Infrastructure) + smaller card (Smart Monitoring Systems) with dark glass look, borders, hover elevation
- **4-column metrics row** — 40% Energy Cost Reduction, ≤30 Days Planning, Real-Time Monitoring, 25 Years Lifecycle
- **GridStack platform section** — Full-width with 4 stacked capability modules (Site Survey, Design & Engineering, Deployment, Monitoring & O&M)
- **Amber CTA section** — "Ready to convert sunlight into infrastructure-grade value?" with Contact button

### Page 2 — Services
- Interlocking grid: large card (Residential Solar, 60%) + side card (Industrial & Commercial, 40%)
- 3 smaller cards below: Battery Storage, Energy Audits, Monitoring & Maintenance
